History of shoes and our collections

It is hard to imagine a time before the invention of shoes. Yet what started as a practical venture has grown into a varied, booming industry just as concerned with art as it is with functionality. Though all shoes share basic characteristics, their coloring, materials, and designs have transformed drastically over thousands of years in the fascinating history of footwear.

In the early seventies, platform shoes and wedges were popular among women, though they became less-so in the eighties and nineties.

If footwear trends continue in this fashion, we can expect the shoes of the future to be even more out-of-this-world.
